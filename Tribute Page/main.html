Mzee Jomo Kenyatta

Freedom fighter, first prime minister and later president of Kenya

Timeline of Mzee Jomo Kenyatta

  • 1891: Born in Gatundu, British East Africa
  • 1908: Enrolled at the Church of Scotland Mission (CSM) at Thogoto
  • 1920: Married Grace Wahu under Kikuyu customs
  • 1922: Worked as a store clerk and water-meter reader for Nairobi Municipal Council
  • 1929: Sent to London to lobby on behalf of Kikuyu tribal land affairs
  • 1963: Became Prime Minister of the autonomous Kenyan government
  • 1978: Served as President until his death
